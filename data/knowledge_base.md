# AutoStream Product Information

## Pricing & Features

### Basic Plan
* **Price**: $29/month
* **Limit**: 10 videos/month
* **Resolution**: 720p resolution
* **Description**: Great for beginners starting their content creation journey.

### Pro Plan
* **Price**: $79/month
* **Limit**: Unlimited videos
* **Resolution**: 4K resolution
* **Features**: Includes AI-generated captions and professional editing tools.

## Company Policies
* **Refund Policy**: No refunds are provided after 7 days of purchase.
* **Customer Support**: 24/7 support is available exclusively for users on the Pro plan.
